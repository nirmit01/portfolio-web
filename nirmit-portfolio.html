📄 IEEE COMSNETS 2025

Federated Knowledge Distillation for Efficient Small Language Models at the Edge

Exploring how knowledge distillation can enable Large Language Model capabilities to be compressed into Small Language Models for deployment on resource-constrained edge devices, while preserving privacy through federated learning. Demonstrated significant inference speedup with minimal accuracy degradation across heterogeneous edge environments.
